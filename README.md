# PDP Compass

## The Essential Decision Tool for Future Filecoin PDP Providers

PDP Compass helps infrastructure operators answer a simple question:

> Should I become a Filecoin PDP Provider?

Running a PDP node is not only a technical decision.

Potential providers need to understand:

- Can my hardware run PDP?
- What are the infrastructure requirements?
- What will it cost me every month?
- What workload assumptions matter?
- What storage price do I need to break even?
- What risks should I understand before participating?

Today, most potential providers can find setup guides and documentation.

What is missing is a practical tool that helps them evaluate whether PDP participation makes economic and operational sense.

PDP Compass is designed to fill that gap.

---

# Why PDP Compass Exists

Filecoin's PDP (Proof of Data Possession) architecture significantly lowers the barrier to providing storage compared to traditional Filecoin sealing-based infrastructure.

However, many potential participants still lack clear answers to practical questions:

- Is my machine suitable?
- How much storage should I start with?
- What are my monthly operating costs?
- What utilization rate is required?
- What pricing assumptions are realistic?
- What is my break-even point?
- What are the biggest risk variables?

Without clear answers, onboarding efforts become inefficient and many potential providers never move forward.

PDP Compass focuses on helping operators make informed decisions before committing hardware, time, and operational resources.

# Who Is This For?

PDP Compass is designed for:

### Existing Filecoin Storage Providers

Operators who already run Filecoin infrastructure and want to evaluate PDP opportunities.

### Former Miners

Machine owners who previously participated in mining networks and are evaluating new infrastructure opportunities.

### Web3 Infrastructure Operators

Teams operating validators, RPC services, indexing infrastructure, or other Web3 infrastructure.

### Small Data Centers & Regional Cloud Providers

Operators with available storage capacity who want to evaluate PDP participation.

### NAS / Homelab Operators

Advanced home infrastructure users who want to understand whether PDP participation is realistic.

### AI Server & Storage Operators

Infrastructure operators supporting AI workloads who are evaluating Filecoin storage opportunities.

# Core Features

## Provider Readiness Assessment

Evaluate whether your infrastructure meets PDP requirements.

Inputs may include:

- CPU
- RAM
- SSD/NVMe
- HDD capacity
- Network connectivity
- Public endpoint availability
- Operational experience

Output:

- Readiness Score
- Missing Requirements
- Recommended Next Steps

---

## Economics Simulator

Estimate:

- Monthly cost
- Storage cost per TB
- Revenue scenarios
- Utilization assumptions
- Break-even pricing
- Payback period

Scenarios include:

- Conservative
- Base
- Optimistic

---

## Provider Personas

PDP Compass includes different provider profiles to reflect real-world differences.

Examples:

- Existing SP
- Former Miner
- Infrastructure Operator
- IDC Operator
- NAS Operator

Each profile has different assumptions and risk characteristics.

---

## Risk Analysis

The simulator highlights key risk variables such as:

- utilization rate
- storage pricing
- bandwidth cost
- hardware depreciation
- uptime requirements

Instead of promising profits, PDP Compass helps users understand where uncertainty comes from.

---

## Methodology Transparency

All calculations are categorized by source type:

| Type | Description |
|--------|--------|
| Official | Filecoin / PDP documentation |
| Market | Hardware and hosting market prices |
| Observed | Real-world operator feedback |
| User-provided | User inputs |
| Assumed | Scenario-based assumptions |

The goal is transparency, not prediction certainty.

# Disclaimer

PDP Compass is a decision-support simulator.

It does not guarantee revenue and should not be considered financial advice.

Results are based on public documentation, market assumptions, user-provided data, observed samples, and scenario modeling.

Actual provider economics may vary significantly depending on workload availability, utilization rates, pricing, hardware cost, bandwidth cost, and future Filecoin ecosystem conditions.
